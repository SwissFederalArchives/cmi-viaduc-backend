  2. Login error

Two-factor authentication required

Your login has failed because you do not have two-factor authentication. Identified users must log in using two-factor authentication. In addition to your e-mail/password, you can use your mobile phone number or an authenticator app, for example:

  • Go to myaccount.eiam.admin.ch.
  • Select your identity provider (e.g. CH-LOGIN) and log in.
  • Choose a second factor to log in (if using CH-LOGIN, in “Login & Security” under “Second Factor Management”).
  • Save your information and log in again at recherche.bar.admin.ch.

You can find detailed instructions for these processes at CH-LOGIN under Adding mTAN as second factor and Adding the authenticator app as second factor.

If you have any questions about your user account, please contact benutzer-admin@bar.admin.ch.
