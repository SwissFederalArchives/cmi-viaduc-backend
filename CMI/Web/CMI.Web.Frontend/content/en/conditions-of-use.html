Conditions of use

The Federal Archives conditions of use govern the use of archive records – whether they are in digital form or consulted in the reading room. They apply to all users.
Articles 9 to 16 of the Federal Act on Archiving (ArchA; SR 152.1) of 26 June 1998 govern access to archive records.
Articles 10 to 19 of the Ordinance to the ArchA (ArchO; SR 152.11) of 8 September 1999 set out specific details relating to those provisions of the Act.

By using archive records in the Federal Archives, you agree to the following:

  1. I accept the Regulations on the use of the Swiss Federal Archives (PDF, 70 kB, 06.03.2008) (German) and undertake to comply with all the provisions thereof, irrespective of where and in what form I consult the archive records or a copy thereof.

  2. I undertake only to exploit dossiers that are freely accessible or that I have received authorisation to consult.

  3. In the event of exploitation or publication, I undertake to comply with any conditions imposed in any authorisations to consult records that have been granted to me.

  4. I undertake to safeguard the personality rights of any persons named in archive records and to comply with the data protection regulations set out in the Federal Act on Data Protection Federal Act on Data Protection (FADP; SR 235.1).

  5. I undertake to comply with the provisions on copyright contained in the Federal Act on Copyright and Related Rights (CopA; SR 231.1).

  6. No commercial use of archive records is permitted without the express written consent of the Swiss Federal Archives.

  7. I acknowledge the Ordinance of the FDHA on the Fees of the Swiss Federal Archives (SFA Fees Ordinance; SR 172.041.15) (German) and accept that fees may be charged for exploi-tation orders.

  8. When using the Swiss Federal Archives WiFi, I accept the BAR-WLAN Conditions of Use (PDF, 177 kB, 14.03.2013).
