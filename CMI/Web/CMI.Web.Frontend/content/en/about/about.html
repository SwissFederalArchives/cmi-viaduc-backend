Online access to the Federal Archives

All documents held by the Swiss Federal Archives can be accessed at recherche.bar.admin.ch. You can use the online access service to search for, order and download dossiers. It also includes advice and information services.

The Swiss Federal Archives holds the documents of the Federal Administration and the political bodies of the Federal State since 1798 and makes them available to anyone interested at recherche.bar.admin.ch. The Fonds page contains a complete overview of the documents in the Federal Archives.

Functions of recherche.bar.admin.ch

recherche.bar.admin.ch includes the full range of services related to accessing the Federal Archives. The main function is the search, which you can also use to order and download documents. The Federal Archives will digitise documents that are still in analogue form to order. In order to use the full range of access services you must create an account .

Contacts

The Federal Archives will be happy to answer any general questions you have. Please use our advice services if you need help using recherche.bar.admin.ch.
