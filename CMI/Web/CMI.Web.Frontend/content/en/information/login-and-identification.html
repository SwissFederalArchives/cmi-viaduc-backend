Login and identification

] You can use the Federal Archives’ online access service anonymously, as a registered user or as an identified person. Depending on your status, the functions available to you will vary.

The possibility to search in publicly accessible metadata is available to all users. To download docu-ments, submit orders and requests or save favourites, you must open a user account. To do this, click on «Login» and follow the instructions under “CH-LOGIN”.

If you wish to search in all metadata or have an authorisation for documents within a closure period, you will need “identified user” status to consult them online. Here is an overview of the rights for each status:

Apply for “identified user” status

You identify yourself via video chat .To do this you need a mobile phone number and a smartphone or computer with a camera and microphone. The video chat will be conducted with an external company (Managed Service Digital Iden-tification & Signing by Swisscom).
