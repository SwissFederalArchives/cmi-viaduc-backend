Citing and publishing

Citing correctly ensures that citations from Federal Archives documents can be traced without difficulty. Anyone wishing to publish documents must comply with the rules of the Federal Archives.

We recommend the following method of citation:

Swiss Federal Archives, E4#1000/781#6*, Az. 2.1, BG vom 23.12.1908 betr. die Organisation des EDI, 1908–1909

The citation consists of the following elements:

  • Institution: Swiss Federal Archives
  • Reference code: E4#1000/781#6*
  • File reference: Az. 2.1
  • Dossier title: BG vom 23.12.1908 betr. die Organisation des EDI
  • Period: 1908–1909

Publishing documents

Reproductions of documents from the fonds of the Federal Archives may be published subject to the following conditions:

  • The documents are not subject to a closure period under Art. 9, 11 or 12 ArchA or the appropriate authorisation has been obtained.
  • Copyright restrictions are complied with. Responsibility for compliance lies with the person publishing the documents.
  • The provisions on personal rights and data protection are complied with (cf. Art. 10, para 2 let. D ArchO). Responsibility for compliance lies with the person publishing the documents.
  • The documents are cited correctly.
  • A specimen copy of the publication is supplied to the Federal Archives.

A written application must be submitted to the Federal Archives before documents are used for commercial purposes. Please send us an e-mail.
