Ordering and consulting

In principle, the fonds of the Federal Archives are publicly accessible. Search services are available online.

You can search, order and consult the fonds online via recherche.bar.admin.ch Instructions on how to search can be found under Searching Suchen.
Consultation of documents from the Federal Archives is generally subject to the conditions of use .
If a dossier is still subject to a closure period under the Federal Act on Archiving (ArchA, SR 152.1), a consultation request is required.

Login and identification

You can use the Federal Archives’ online access anonymously, as a registered user or as an identi-fied person. Subject to status, you will have different functions available for your search. For more in-formation see Registration and identification. Before you can order and consult documents you must at least register.

Shopping cart

Add documents you wish to order to the shopping cart. They will be automatically divided into two categories:
  1. 1. Freely accessible documents and documents for which the Federal Archives must review acces-sibility are listed under “Order documents”.
  2. 2. If the dossiers are still subject to a closure period they will appear under “Submit consultation re-quest”. For this category you must first submit a request to consult records.

Some documents are not described online. To order them, use the “Add dossiers to the shopping cart manually” function.
If you have a previous authorisation to consult the documents concerned, enter its date in the “Author-isation already available” field.

Consulting documents

You can consult documents from the Federal Archives digitally at recherche.bar.admin.ch or in ana-logue form in the reading rooms. The Federal Administration can also use the administration loan ser-vice.

Consulting online

Digitising a dossier takes two working days. If you have ordered more than one dossier, you will then receive one per day. As soon as the dossier has been digitised and is ready, you will be notified by e-mail. Documents that are already available in digital form can be consulted directly online.

Consulting in the reading room

You can also consult analogue documents in the reading room, but you must order them in advance; please note our opening hours. Twelve containers will be supplied per person and day. Orders received by 3 p.m. will be delivered within an hour. Orders received after 3 p.m. or outside general opening hours will be delivered within one hour of the time when the reading room next opens.

Administration loans

Article 14 ArchA allows federal bodies that accession records to consult their own records during the closure period. On request, analogue documents that have been ordered will be sent by registered mail. Documents can also be consulted in the Federal Archives reading rooms. We recommend this option especially for large orders. Digital documents are normally made available in digital form.
